Octobre 10, 2025

I am ashamed.<br>
I make mistakes.<br>
For I am just a human.<br>
I don't want to hide,<br>
Or run away from accountability.<br>
But I strive to be virtuous.<br>
So I confront my feelings, with an open heart.<br>
Jumping into the cold water, a leap of faith.
