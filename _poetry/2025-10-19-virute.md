Octobre 10, 2025

I am ashamed.
I make mistakes.
For I am just a human.
I don't want to hide,
Or run away from accountability.
But I strive to be virtuous.
So I confront my feelings, with an open heart.
Jumping into the cold water, a leap of faith.
